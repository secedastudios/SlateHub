Setting up your app

  1. Create or join an organization on Slatehub.
  2. From your org profile, click the gear icon to open Settings.
  3. Under API & Integrations, click Enable OIDC. Save your client secret — it's shown only once.
  4. Add your app's redirect URIs.
  5. Configure your client library with the discovery URL above.
