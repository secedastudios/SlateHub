The Fine Print

So How Is This Free?

Profiles, search, networking, jobs, and community tools are free for everyone—forever. That's not a launch promo. That's the model.

We make money by selling production management tools—scheduling, budgeting, call sheets, and collaboration features built for productions with real budgets. The people who can afford to pay are the ones running funded projects, not the actors and crew trying to get hired.

Your profile and visibility will never be held behind a paywall. That's a promise we put in our open‑source code.
