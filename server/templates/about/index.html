About Us

The Free Home for Filmmakers, Actors, Crew, and Creators

No subscriptions. No ads. No hidden algorithms. Open source and built by the community.

Why We Built This

We got tired of paying platforms that don't work for us. Casting sites that charge actors $25/month just to be seen. Crew boards with opaque ranking systems. Freelancer marketplaces that take a cut and don't understand production roles. So we built our own—free for everyone, forever.

What We Believe

Talent Shouldn't Have a Price Tag

Your visibility should never depend on a subscription. Profiles, search, networking, jobs, and community tools are free—and stay that way.

Every Format. One Community.

Feature films, TV series, YouTube channels, streaming originals, branded content, podcasts—one platform for everyone who makes video.

Transparency Over Algorithms

No secret rankings. No pay-to-rank. SlateHub is open source—you can see exactly how the platform works and help shape it.

What You Get

01

One Profile for Everything

Your reels, credits, headshots, skills, and experience in one verified profile. No more splitting your career across five platforms.

02

Smart Search

Describe who you need in plain English—by skill, location, experience, or look—and get matched instantly. No keyword games.

03

Productions & Crew Management

Create productions, invite cast and crew, manage roles and credits—all from one place.

04

Job Board

Post and discover opportunities across the creative industry. No fees to post, no fees to apply.

05

Organizations & Communities

Film schools, production companies, collectives, and local film communities can manage members, share opportunities, and build their presence.

06

Verified Profiles

One-time verification ensures real people with confirmed credits. No fake casting calls, no spam, no ghost accounts.
